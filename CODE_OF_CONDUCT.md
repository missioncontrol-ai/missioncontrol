# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to a harassment-free, respectful community experience for all
participants.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and professional.
- Give and receive feedback constructively.
- Assume good intent, but prioritize user and system safety.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory or hateful language.
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or doxxing.
- Disruptive behavior that blocks healthy collaboration.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that
violate this policy.

## Reporting
Report incidents privately via the contact methods in `SECURITY.md` or through
repository maintainers.
